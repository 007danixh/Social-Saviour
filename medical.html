We are providing the care you need

While the divide between poor and rich is ever-growing and the fact that inflation is actually adding fuel to the fire, some of us are not able to even fulfill our basic needs. In such situations, health scenario of our valley is deteriorating day by day. The people who are not financially doing well are not able to meet their medical expenses, be it the cost of medicines, the consultation fees of doctors or the diagnostic charges. It is the responsibility of every conscious Kashmiri to help those people.

We, by the grace of Allah (SWT), at Social Saviour try to do our bit by helping them. We register the families with us after verifying them by sending a team of 2 male people who make sure that only deserving people get the resources. The verification is done in two steps:

An on-spot verification is done by our team.
